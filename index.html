What is StudySesh?

StudySesh is a project that aims to provide students with all the resources they need to have a successful study session in one place.

Majority of websites are text heavy about many different study methods, while others are specialised to one method. Therefore, StudySesh aims to bridge this gap by providing the resources and tools to multiple methods, while keeping the information easy to read and understand.
